We are AZADians!

As Azadians, not giving up is an important trait that lets us achieve our goals and overcome obstacles in life. We offer a listening ear, a shoulder to cry on, and words of encouragement, and stand together united always.

Creative Minds

We believe that creativity is essential for personal growth, innovation, and progress in society. Creativity enables individuals to think outside the box, find new and innovative solutions to problems, and approach challenges from a different perspective.

Friendship

Azadians provide emotional support to each other in times of need, such as during a difficult situation or when facing a challenge. They offer a listening ear, a shoulder to cry on, and words of encouragement, which can help reduce stress and improve mental health.

Social & Cultural

Social and Cultural events are an essential part of our identity. These ensure that people can connect and celebrate the traditions, beliefs, and customs, creating a sense of belonging, pride, and unity.

Consistency & Perseverance

We believe in hard work being the foundation of success, and it is often the determining factor that sets successful people apart from those who are not. We understand that success is not a one-time event but rather the result of a consistent effort over time.

Alumni Network

The strong alumni network fosters a sense of community and pride among Azadians. Networking helps use expand our professional network and stay up-to-date on industry news and trends.

About

Azad Hall of Residence is named after India's first education minister, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad. It is famous for having started the tradition of Illumination, or ILLU as today's KGPians know it. Azad Hall is always reputed for its stellar performances in Soc-Cult events, especially Dramatics and Choreography.

Details

  • Founded: 3rd March 1954
  • Motto: प्रणिपातेन परिप्रश्नेन सेवया
  • Warden: Prof. Nikhil Kumar Singha
  • Assistant. Warden(s):Prof. Abir Chakravorty (Mess)
    Prof. Mrinal Kanti Mandal (Maintenance)
  • Hall President: Mr . Ambuj Sharma
  • SSM: Mr. Kartik Jain
  • Capacity: 600+
  • Sharing: Single, Double, Triple

There are a total of 424 rooms for the accommodation of the students of Azad Hall, with 260 Single rooms, 20 Double sharing rooms, 142 Triple sharing rooms and 2 Four sharing rooms. Also, the Hall provides 2 Double-bedded AC guest rooms with television and attached bathrooms having geyser installed.

Hall Council Members

Hall Council members are selected each year by the warden of the hall to ensure smooth conduction of hall related activitiies within the hall. These activities include Hall maintenance, welfare activities, grand dinners, festival functions, alumni meets, etc. Hierarchically, the Hall President and Second Senate Member(4th years) work in coordination with the General Secretaries(3rd years) and the secretaries(2nd years) to ensure the smooth functioning of the hall.
